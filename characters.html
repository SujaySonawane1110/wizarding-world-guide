The Golden Trio

Harry Potter

Known as "The Boy Who Lived," Harry James Potter is the only known survivor of the Killing Curse. Physically, he resembles his father, James, but possesses his mother Lily's bright green eyes. Marked by a lightning bolt scar, he sacrificed himself to destroy the Horcrux within him and defeated Lord Voldemort.

House: Gryffindor
Wand: 11", Holly, Phoenix Feather
Patronus: Stag

Hermione Granger

Often described as the "brightest witch of her age," Hermione Jean Granger is a Muggle-born witch with bushy brown hair and a logical, encyclopedic mind. She is the brains behind the Trio. She eventually became the Minister for Magic, revolutionizing wizarding law.

House: Gryffindor
Wand: 10¾", Vine, Dragon Heartstring
Patronus: Otter

Ron Weasley

Ronald Bilius Weasley is the sixth son of the Weasley family. He provides the heart and humor of the group. Though he often struggled with insecurity living in the shadow of his famous best friend, his loyalty ultimately prevailed.

House: Gryffindor
Wand: 14", Willow, Unicorn Hair
Patronus: Jack Russell Terrier

Hogwarts Teachers & Staff

Albus Dumbledore

Albus Percival Wulfric Brian Dumbledore was the Headmaster of Hogwarts. Tall, thin, and very old, he radiated power and benevolence. He founded the Order of the Phoenix and possessed the Elder Wand.
